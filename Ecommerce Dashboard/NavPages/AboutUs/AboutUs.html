Our Story

Founded with a passion for quality and craftsmanship, Leathers is dedicated to creating premium leather goods that blend style, durability, and functionality. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name, offering a curated selection of jackets, handbags, travel bags, keychains, sunglass cases, and shoes, all crafted with the finest materials. Our mission is to provide timeless pieces that elevate your style and accompany you on every journey.

Our Craftsmanship

Every stitch tells a story. Our artisans use time-honored techniques combined with modern precision to create leather goods that stand the test of time. We source only the highest-quality leather, ensuring each product is as durable as it is beautiful. From hand-stitched jackets to meticulously crafted sunglass cases, our commitment to excellence shines through in every detail.
